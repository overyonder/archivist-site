01 — WHAT IS ARCHIVIST?

Not another launcher.
The system behind your collection.

Most frontends begin when your library is already clean. Archivist begins at the source: it understands manifests, applies preservation policy, verifies every file, manages emulator intent, and turns the result into a living map of game history.
