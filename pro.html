One title, not five silos.

No-Intro, Redump, TOSEC, MAME and TDC can each describe valid material while still leaving the same game scattered across providers, systems, revisions and formats. Archivist Pro applies hand-reviewed cross-provider decisions to choose one authoritative identity and its preferred representations.

Plain answers

Before you choose.

Can I keep using Archivist Free indefinitely?

Yes. Free users can continue managing, checking and playing their collections. Pro adds proprietary curation and historical interpretation instead of removing an artificial limit.

What happens when my content-update year ends?

Nothing is taken away. You keep Pro, every software update, and the latest Canon and Atlas content released during your entitlement. Renew only when subsequent research is worth it to you.

Is Archivist a subscription?

No. The initial plan is a perpetual US$40 Pro licence. Curator and Historian updates can be renewed manually for another year; automatic subscriptions and lifetime-content plans are not part of the initial offer.

Does Archivist include commercial games?

No. Archivist configures and manages the game software you legally obtain. It does not supply copyrighted games.

Can I buy Founding Pro now?

Not yet. Checkout will remain disconnected until the usable build, founding discount, delivery promise, support process and refund terms can all be stated clearly.
